Paul - Guitar

Born in South Africa, my journey with Christ began as a frustrated teenager, convinced that adults had set everything up to suit themselves. I was left with a choice: either follow Him and help build His Kingdom, or go to war with society.

Today, I am an actuary and data science professional working in financial services. Our family lives and breathes music, and every pursuit in my life has been shaped by the incredible presence of God.

In my world, God is the most amazing guitarist, vocalist, songwriter, business professional, and everything else imaginable!
