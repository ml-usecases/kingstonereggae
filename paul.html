Paul - Guitar

As a young man I faced a choice: either choose Christ and help to build His Kingdom, or oppose everything I saw as the establishment. Since then, His presence and provision have defined the trajectory of my life, and the life of my family, in powerful ways.

I work in financial services. Our family lives and breathes music, and every pursuit in my life has been shaped by the incredible presence of God.

In my world, God is the most amazing guitarist, vocalist, songwriter, business professional, and everything else imaginable!
